Outreach

Physics Club is an after school tutoring and outreach program at Democracy Prep Harlem High School hosted by the Columbia Physics department. Due to covid-19, this fall season is going to consist of informal talks about physics/research/career path and extensive Q&A with the students. Eventually, this program will consist of a more holistic approach towards STEM education and getting kids involved in research. If you are interested and would like to know more, feel free to check out their website found by clicking on the image to the left.


CompileHer is a student organization founded at the University of Chicago that aims to engage young girls from all across the city through computer science education, adopting an approach to our programming that is necessarily intersectional in nature. CompileHer was created to ensure the field of tech serves as an equalizing force for social mobility that is not limited by barriers to educational opportunity along racial, socioeconomic, and gender lines. Broadly, the organizational objective is to break down those barriers: we are committed to closing the gender gap in the tech world by providing free, high-quality computer science education to young women.


UCQuantum is a student run organization looking to create a space on the University of Chicago's campus to learn, discuss, and explore the field of Quantum Computation. Students of all backgrounds are invited to learn about and discuss contemporary questions that are broadly related to quantum computing. It serves as a liason between the scientific pursuits that are happening on campus and the wider public who would like to learn more.


About Me

I am an experimental physicist pursuing my PhD at Columbia University. Currently, my interests are primarily in pursuing a novel quantum computing platform using neutral Strontium in an atomic array. I remain interested in both academic as well as private quantum computing pursuits. In addition to my research, I am a strong advocate for improving STEM education and diversity in physics.
