About Me

I am an experimental physicist currently pursuing my PhD at Columbia University. As a member of the Will Lab, I am pursuing a novel quantum computing platform using neutral Strontium for the qubits. I remain interested in both academic as well as private quantum computing pursuits. In addition to my research, I am a strong advocate for STEM education and increasing diversity in physics.
